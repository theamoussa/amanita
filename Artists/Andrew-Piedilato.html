Biography

Andrew Piedilato (b. 1974) was born in Athens, GA and now lives and works in Brooklyn, NY. He is the recipient of the 2015 Rosenthal Family Foundation Award in Painting. He also received the George Segal Grant in 2005, and was nominated for the Lambent Fellowship in 2006. Andrew received his MFA in Painting from Pratt Institute, NY and has a BFA in Painting from the University of Georgia, GA. Andrew has been in solo and group shows at The American Academy of Arts and Letters, New York; Patrick Painter, Los Angeles; HVCCA, English Kills, and Black & White Gallery, New York. A selection of public collections include Berkeley Art Museum, Fredrick R. Weisman Museum of Art, Flint Institute of Arts, and the Pacific Film Archive
