Arianna Tosi

Biography

Arianna Tosi (1994) was born in Omegna, Italy. She has a degree from the Academy of Fine Arts in Florence and a Master of Fine Arts in painting from the Academy of Brera in Milan.

Arianna Tosi paints using air-brush and spray paint. The choice of these histrionic mediums is incongruent to the harmonious nature of the work. This initial contradiction informs the grand scheme of her practice.
