WHO WE ARE

Amanita was founded in 2021 with the primary mission of supporting and exhibiting artists at various stages of their careers. Since inception, we have mounted over two dozen exhibitions between our gallery spaces in Florence and New York as well as in non-permanent spaces in Milan, St. Moritz, Miami, and Los Angeles.

Our New York gallery features 3,500 square feet of exhibition space in the location formerly home to CBGB, the historic punk-rock nightclub. Situated on the Bowery in Manhattan’s East Village neighborhood, the gallery’s museum-quality finishes combined with the still-permeating energies of CBGB make for a singular viewing experience. The layout of the gallery lends itself to various formats and possibilities for our exhibitions. Amanita New York also includes studios for artists to work in-situ, providing two studio spaces in the gallery’s basement.

The gallery’s artist first approach allows us to provide ample opportunities for artists both in the preparation, realization, and exhibition of their artistic output.

The gallery is operated by its founders, Caio Twombly, Tommaso Rositani Suckert, Jacob Hyman, and Garrett Goldsmith as well as its small team of directors and registrars between New York and Italy.
