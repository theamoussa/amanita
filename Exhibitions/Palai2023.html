Overview

Amanita participated in the 2023 edition of Palai, exhibiting works by Francesco Cima, Leonardo Meoni and Daniele Milvio.

Palai opened July 28th at Palazzo Tamborini Cezzi in Lecce, Italy. The exhibition comprised over seventy works from a selection of international galleries and artists.

Participating galleries: 15 orient (New York), Amanita (New York), Barbara Weiss (Berlin), Balice Hertling (Paris), Bel Ami (Los Angeles), Delgosha (Tehran), Ermes Ermes (Rome), HUA International (Beijing / Berlin), Isla Flotante (Buenos Aires), LC Queisser (Tblisi), Tara Downs (New York)
