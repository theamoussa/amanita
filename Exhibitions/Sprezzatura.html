Sprezzatura

Via dei Sassetti 1, Florence, Italy
February 19, 2021 - May 1, 2021

Overview

Sprezzatura marks Amanita's first ever exhibition and was inaugurated in Florence, Italy on February 19, 2021. The exhibition is the product of a collaboration with Avant Arte and presented works by 14 international artists.
