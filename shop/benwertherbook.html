When You Can No Longer Speak, Sing Me a Song is a photo mockumentary project documenting the subculture of military simulation, airsoft, and live action role play throughout the US.

Ben Werther is an American artist who received his BFA from Cooper Union in 2020 and has been developing his practice through an interdisciplinary approach that questions the nature of belief, knowledge, and memory.
